Accommodation

***September 15 News:

The CHEP 2016 room block at the Marriott Marquis is now FULL.

If you really want to stay in the conference hotel, rooms may be availble by calling the Marriott at +1 415-896-1600.  The cost per night is likely to be significantly higher than that of the room block.

San Francisco offers many accommodation possibilities within walking distance of the conference hotel.  You are encouraged to look at the many web sites (hotels.com, airbnb, gocalifornia ...) that offer accommodation. [The conference organizers do not recommend or endorse any particular site, of course].

If you plan on walking at night, practice general safety and be aware of your surroundings.
