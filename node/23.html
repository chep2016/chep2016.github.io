Travel

Car rental is NOT Recommended.  San Francisco is a compact city, public transport is good and parking charges are high to astronomical.
 

From San Francisco International Airport SFO

  • Take BART train from SFO to the Powell Street Station
    • trains every 15 minutes,
    • 31 minutes (29 minutes train + two minutes walk to the Marriott Marquis)
    • about $9
  • Taxi, Uber, etc.  see rideguru.com ($31 upwards)

 

From Oakland International Airport OAK

  • Take BART  train to Coliseum, then BART train to Powell Street Station
    • trains every 6 minutes
    • 41 minutes (8 minutes train + wait + 23 minutes train + two minutes walk to the Marriott)
    • about $10.20
  • Taxi, Uber, etc.  see rideguru.com ($31 upwards)

 

From San Jose International Airport SJC

  • Vatious multi-hop public transport options (try "San Jose SJC to Mariott Marquis SF" in google maps).
    • about 2 hours
    • less than $15
  • Taxi, Uber, etc.  see rideguru.com ($62 upwards)

 

Parking
If you are driving, we suggest the off-site parking next door to the hotel at 5th & Mission Garage: http://www.fifthandmission.com/home.htm
Fee: 3.50 USD hourly, 34 USD daily

Alternately, the hotel offers Valet-only parking for $70.70 USD daily, Oversized USD $82.10. Garage clearance 6 feet. Use 780 Mission St as address for directions.
